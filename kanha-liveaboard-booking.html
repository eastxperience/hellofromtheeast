3D2N Standard Route

Three days, eight iconic stops.

Day 1 · Departure day
Labuan Bajo → Kelor → Rinca → Kalong (overnight)
Board at the dock, cold welcome drinks. Sail to Kelor for the first hike and snorkel. Rinca Island ranger-guided dragon trek. At sunset, anchor off Kalong as 100,000 flying foxes fill the sky. Dinner at anchor — bioluminescence in the water.
Day 2 · Travel day onboard
Padar Sunrise → Pink Beach → Taka Makassar → Manta Point (overnight)
5 AM hike up Padar before every other boat. Breakfast underway. Pink Beach for swimming and paddleboards. Sandbar sunset at Taka Makassar. Golden-hour snorkel with mantas at Manta Point. Long deck dinner under the Milky Way.
Day 3 · Travel day onboard
Sebayur → Kanawa → Labuan Bajo
Morning snorkel at Sebayur healthy reef. Final swim at Kanawa. Lunch while sailing home. Dock at Labuan Bajo by 15:00, transfer back to hotel or airport.
