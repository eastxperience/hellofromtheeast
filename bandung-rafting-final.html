Questions, answered

The things every traveler asks.

Do I need experience?
None. 95% of our guests are first-timers. Two certified guides per raft walk everyone through paddle commands before launch.
What's the minimum age?
8 years. Kids under 12 need to be able to swim with a life vest. Seniors up to 70 regularly join.
What do I bring?
Swimwear, change of clothes, towel, sunblock, sense of humor. We provide everything else including a waterproof bag for your dry gear.
What if it rains?
Light rain = perfect rafting conditions. Heavy storms = we reschedule or refund fully. Call is made 24h in advance.
Is photo/video included?
Yes — GoPro photos and edited highlight clips sent via WhatsApp within 48 hours. Dedicated videographer available as a +IDR 500K add-on.
Can vegetarians / halal guests eat the lunch?
Absolutely. Tell us at booking. Full vegetarian and halal versions of the Sundanese banquet available — same quality, same portions.
