Questions, answered

Real sailing questions.

Who's typically on the boat?
Mostly independent travelers aged 22–40 — solo travelers, couples, small friend groups. Mix of English/European/Asian origins. We screen-match every booking to keep the group chemistry right.
Is it a party boat?
No. It's social. Good conversations, great food, live acoustic guitar, wine — but not a club. If you want Gili-style parties, this isn't the right boat. If you want meaningful nights with good people, it is.
Can I come solo?
Over 50% of guests do. Twin-share matches you with a same-gender vetted traveler. Or take a Private Cabin for solo comfort. Either way, by dinner on day 1 you're part of the group.
What about seasickness?
Komodo waters are mostly calm (especially Apr–Nov). We sail mostly at anchor or at low speed. Seasickness meds available onboard; take them 1h before departure if you're sensitive.
What food is served?
9 meals total. Breakfast: pancakes, eggs, fruit, pastries, coffee. Lunch/dinner: fresh grilled fish, Indonesian/Asian fusion, salads, vegetables, desserts. Vegetarian, halal, gluten-free all standard. Tell us at booking.
Is there Wi-Fi? Can I charge devices?
Patchy mobile data in parts. Every cabin has charging, boat has a lounge charging bar. We encourage 3 days offline — most guests say it's the best part.
What about sustainability?
Reef-safe sunscreen provided, no single-use plastic, stainless flasks onboard, direct park conservation fee included. Boat uses sail when wind permits to cut fuel use.
Cancellation policy?
Full refund 30+ days out. 50% refund 14–29 days out. Inside 14 days: transferable to a future departure (or another guest). Full refund if we cancel due to weather.
