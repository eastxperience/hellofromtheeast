Village Etiquette
  • Cover shoulders and knees inside the village.
  • Ask before photographing anyone, especially elders and children.
  • Do not enter ceremonial areas without invitation.
  • Follow your guide during any ritual or gathering.
  • Carry all waste out. No single-use plastics above Denge.
