— Questions

Before you write us.

Is payment safe? How do I pay?+

Yes. We accept bank transfer and major payment methods. You'll receive a clear invoice with itemized costs before any payment. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Why book direct instead of through a platform?+

Platforms aggregate inventory. We operate it. Direct booking means you speak to the person who decides your departure, customises your itinerary, and answers in under 5 minutes — not a ticket queue. The rate is also better because there's no commission layer.

Do you speak English? Other languages?+

Fluent English is our standard. Our team also speaks Indonesian and basic Mandarin. All communication is fast, clear, and friendly.

What's your refund & cancellation policy?+

Flexible cancellation windows depending on the trip. We provide clear terms upfront — no fine print, no gotchas. Plans change, we understand.

Is hotel pickup included?+

Yes, most trips include hotel pickup and drop-off within the service area. We confirm all logistics before your trip so you just relax.

Are there any hidden fees?+

Zero. The price we quote is the price you pay. National park entrance fees and specific extras are always listed upfront.

How fast can you plan my trip?+

WhatsApp reply in under 5 minutes. Full custom itinerary proposal within hours. We've had guests book and travel within 48 hours.

Can you handle groups and families?+

From couples to 30+ person groups, multi-generational families, and private buyouts. Tell us your pace, we design around it.
